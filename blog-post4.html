Questions to Ask Before Signing with an Agency

Hiring an agency can be exciting — but rushing in without asking the right questions can cost you time, money, and a lot of frustration. Before you say yes, here are a few smart questions to ask. These will help you find the right partner and avoid surprises later.

1. What exactly will you do for me?

Make sure the agency explains their services in simple terms. You should understand:

  • What tasks they’ll handle
  • What’s included and what’s not
  • What success will look like

Don’t assume — ask clearly.

2. Can I see your past work?

A good agency will proudly show examples of what they’ve done before. Look for:

  • Projects similar to yours
  • Results they’ve achieved
  • How they solve real problems

If they can’t show anything — that’s a red flag.

3. Who will I work with directly?

Sometimes you speak with the founder or a sales rep, but once the contract is signed, you’re handed off to someone else. Ask:

  • Who’s managing your project day-to-day?
  • Will you have regular check-ins?
  • Is there one point of contact?

Communication makes or breaks projects.

4. What is your process like?

You don’t need every detail, but you should know:

  • How do they plan and track progress?
  • How are changes or delays handled?
  • What tools or systems will they use?

A clear process = fewer surprises.

5. How do you charge, and what will I pay?

Talk about money upfront. Ask:

  • Is it a one-time fee or monthly?
  • Are there any extra or hidden costs?
  • What happens if the work takes longer than expected?

Good agencies are transparent about pricing.

6. What if things go wrong?

No one likes to think about it — but things can go off-track. Ask:

  • What happens if you’re not happy with the work?
  • Is there a refund or revision policy?
  • How do they handle disagreements?

Knowing this now avoids stress later.

7. Can I talk to one of your past clients?

Hearing from someone who worked with them before helps you know what to expect. You don’t need a long interview — even a short review or testimonial helps.

8. How flexible are you?

Every business is different. You want an agency that:

  • Can adjust if your needs change
  • Is open to feedback
  • Doesn’t treat you like “just another client”

The best agencies feel like an extension of your team.

✅ Final Thought

Agencies can do amazing work — but only when the partnership is built on trust, clear communication, and the right expectations. Before signing anything, take a little time to ask these questions. Your future self will thank you!
