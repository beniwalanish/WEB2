How We Onboard Agencies On Agency Bazar

Application Submission


Agencies start by submitting an application form on our website. They provide essential details such as:

🔍


Company name & registration info

📈


Services offered

📊


Team size and structure

🧪


Website and portfolio links

📉


Contact details and key personnel

Criteria-Based Evaluation


We don’t list just any agency. Each one must meet specific core criteria:

🔍


Proven Track Record

We look for case studies, real client work, and outcomes. An agency must show a history of successfully delivering projects.

📈


Niche or Specialized Expertise

We prefer agencies that excel in specific areas (e.g., SaaS marketing, Shopify development, healthcare UX) rather than being generalists.

📊


Clear Communication

Communication is a key success factor. We review how responsive, transparent, and professional the agency is in interactions.

🧪


Verified Identity & Legitimacy

We check business credentials, social proof, and legal existence to ensure legitimacy.

📉


Ethical Practices

We do not list agencies involved in shady SEO practices, misleading advertising, or plagiarized portfolios.

Review Call


To connect with the agency, we conduct a short review call. This helps us assess:

🔍


Culture fit

📈


Team structure

📊


Pricing transparency

🧪


Workflow process

📉


Commitment to quality

Ongoing Monitoring & Reviews


We’re not a “list and forget” platform. Agency Bazar conducts:

🔍


Periodic quality checks

📈


Project performance reviews

📊


Client feedback analysis

🧪


Blacklisting for consistent poor performance or ethics violations

Why This Matters


For startups and businesses using Agency Bazar, this process means:

🔍


Less risk

📈


Higher quality outcomes

📊


Faster, smoother hiring

🧪


Better alignment with business goals
